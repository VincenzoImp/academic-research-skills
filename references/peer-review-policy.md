# Peer Review Policy

Use this reference for adversarial review and rebuttal.

## Review Dimensions

- novelty
- significance
- correctness
- methodology
- related work coverage
- clarity
- reproducibility
- ethics and broader impact
- fit to venue

## Concern Severity

- `fatal`: likely rejection unless fixed.
- `major`: important weakness that affects acceptance.
- `minor`: fixable without changing the main claim.
- `presentation`: clarity, organization, notation, or style.

## Concern Record

- `concern_id`
- `reviewer_or_source`
- `severity`
- `paper_location`
- `claim_or_issue`
- `evidence`
- `recommended_action`
- `response_status`

## Rebuttal Actions

- `concede`
- `clarify`
- `add-evidence`
- `reframe`
- `defend`
- `defer-to-future-work`

Good rebuttals answer the exact concern and promise only changes that can appear
in the revised paper or artifact.
