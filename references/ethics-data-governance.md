# Ethics Data Governance

Use this reference for research involving people, public web traces, sensitive
data, platform content, sensitive-domain material, or restricted sources.

## Governance Record

Maintain `docs/ethics/data-governance.md` with:

- project data categories
- data subjects or affected groups
- collection method and boundaries
- consent or public-interest rationale
- IRB/ethics-board status when relevant
- terms-of-service and license constraints
- retention and deletion policy
- sharing and publication policy
- redaction or aggregation rules

## Sensitivity Classes

- `public-low-risk`
- `public-contextual-risk`
- `personal-data`
- `sensitive-personal-data`
- `restricted-or-harmful-content`
- `proprietary-or-licensed`
- `dual-use`

## Publication Defaults

Prefer aggregate statistics, anonymized examples, synthetic snippets, and
redacted screenshots. Keep raw sensitive data out of git and out of published
artifacts unless there is a documented approval path.
