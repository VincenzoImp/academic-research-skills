# Research Positioning Policy

Use this reference for ideas, contribution claims, novelty, and paper framing.

## Contribution Claim Fields

- `claim_id`
- `claim`
- `contribution_type`
- `closest_prior_work`
- `delta_from_prior_work`
- `evidence_needed`
- `current_evidence`
- `failure_condition`
- `target_audience`
- `target_venue`
- `status`

## Claim Status

- `hypothesis`
- `plausible`
- `partially-supported`
- `supported`
- `overstated`
- `rejected`

## Novelty Tests

- Has the closest prior work been named?
- Is the delta specific and meaningful?
- Would a reviewer from the target venue care?
- Can the claim fail under a plausible experiment or source?
- Is the contribution type explicit?

## Writing Rule

The paper narrative must not be stronger than the current evidence plan.
